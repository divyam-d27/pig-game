GAME RULES‼️

💥 Your Turn:
🔶 Roll the dice.
🔶 You can roll again (as many times as you want) to add the new number to your current roll total.

💥 Hold or Roll?
🔶 Hold: If you're happy with your total, click "hold" to keep the points you rolled this turn. These points are added to your overall score.
🔶 Roll: If you want to risk getting a higher score, roll again. Remember, if you roll a 1 at any point during your turn, ALL points from that turn are lost.

💥 Winning: Be the first player to reach a total score of 100 points.
